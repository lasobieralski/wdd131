Welcome to SugarSense

Resources at your fingertips

My purpose is to create a supportive and empowering online resource for individuals managing diabetes and anyone seeking a healthier, more balanced lifestyle. Inspired by personal experience and guided by faith, I believe that small, mindful steps can lead to transformative changes. Through "SugarSense," I aim to provide:

  • Practical Tools: Empower users with tools to track weight and blood sugar levels, fostering greater awareness and control over their health. By integrating tracking features, users can celebrate progress and stay motivated on their wellness journey.
  • Diabetic-Friendly Recipes: Share a curated collection of recipes tailored to meet the nutritional needs of diabetics, emphasizing low-sugar and low-carb options while exploring plant-based and vegetarian alternatives. These recipes reflect the principle of moderation and the belief that healthy eating can be both nourishing and enjoyable.
  • Faith-Based Encouragement: Offer inspiration rooted in scripture, drawing from passages like Genesis 1:29 and Daniel 1 in the Bible as well as Doctrine and Covenants section 89:10-16, which remind us of the value of natural, wholesome foods. Through faith-filled reflections and resources, I aim to nurture both physical and spiritual well-being.
  • Educational Support: Provide clear, practical guidance and information to help users make informed dietary and lifestyle choices. Foster a sense of community where learning is shared, and personal growth is celebrated.
  • Health and Balance: Promote the idea that managing diabetes—and life as a whole—is a journey of balance. By taking consistent steps in nutrition, health tracking, and self-awareness, users can achieve a healthier, more fulfilling life.

"SugarSense" is more than a website—it's a community inspired by personal growth and guided by faith. It reflects a belief in health, balance, and compassion, offering tools and resources to empower others on similar paths. Together, we can make better choices, live more abundantly, and find joy in the journey toward wellness.
