Overview

Purpose

My purpose is to create a supportive and empowering online resource for individuals managing diabetes. Through "SugarSense," I aim to provide: pracical tools to track weight and blood sugar levels, daibetic-friednly recipes, educational support by offering guidance and information to help users make informed nutritionaland lifestyle choices for better health. My vision is deeply rooted in personal experience, making "SugarSense" an authentic, compassionate, and practical platform for others on a similar journey.

Audience

At SugarSense, my goal is to empower diabetics and anyone seeking a healthier, more balanced lifestyle. Through personalized tools, delicious recipes, and practical guidance, I aim to support everyone on their journey toward better nutrition and well-being.

Dynamic elements

Recipe Search & Filter: Interactive search by category or name. Weight & Sugar Tracker: Logs progress with real-time graphs and feedback. Nutritional Calculator: Computes totals for meal plans. Educational Tips: Displays rotating tips on healthy habits. Feedback Messages: Personalized encouragement and suggestions.

Branding

Website Logo

Here is a logo combining a soft green droplet with a leaf, hexagonal honeycomb pattern, and a circular shape to convey natural healing, balance, and harmony. The stylized font conveys a modern yet approachable feel, emphasizing the site’s focus on empowering users to manage their health through nutrition and tracking.

Content

Home page

The home page will introduce SugarSense, explain its purpose (supporting diabetics and promoting healthy nutrition), and showcase the following features:

  • A welcome message to engage users and explain the site’s purpose.
  • A navigation bar for easy access to site sections.
  • A randomly displayed featured recipe with an image and description.
  • A search bar for users to look up recipes by name or description.
  • A footer with social media links.

Search and Share Recipes

This page will allow users to:

  • Search for recipes by name or description using a search bar.
  • Filter recipes by tags to quickly find specific categories (e.g., Low-Carb, Dessert).
  • Submit their favorite recipes through a user-friendly form.

Tracking and Resources

This page will include tools and tips for managing diabetes:

  • A section to track weight and blood sugar levels, displayed with a dynamic graph.
  • Educational articles about diabetic-friendly nutrition and lifestyle tips.
  • Links to trusted external resources for diabetes management.
